Frequent Asked Questions

Q: How do I rate or write a review about a company/office?
A: Simply sign up a SIVIS account, and you will be able to rate or write a review about a company or an office, go to the page of the company/office, and then click "Rate" for a simply rating or "Write Review" for a rating with comments and reviews.
Q: Is my rating or review anonymous?
A: When you simply rate a company/office, all ratings will be anonymous, i.e. no one can see your own ratings of a company/office except yourself. When you write reviews about a company/office, your username will be shown as the commentor, however, if you choose to use your full name in account settings, your full name will be shown as the commentor instead of your username.
Q: Can I change my rating about a company?
A: An user could only rate a same company/office once, however, you can revise your ratings or reviews by simply click revise review.
Q: Why does SIVIS categorize a registered company as just an office?
A: On SIVIS site, company and office are the only two type of a firm, company is for a company/firm/representative office which make commercial and chartering decisions independently or the headoffice of a conglomerate group; office on SIVIS might be a registered company, however, it is closely cooperated with its headquarter or parent company and work as a branch in shipping world.
Q: Will the rating and review on a company/office affect its parent company or subsidary company/office?
A: Rating and reviews of an office or a company will copy the same to its parent company, but won't affect its subsidiary company or sister company.
Q: Can I put my business on this site?
A: Yes, you can put your own business on this site, presently we are still testing our website so the DIY prcedure isn't currently available, however, you can contact us via email and we will add your company on our site, and it's totally free.
